Refund Policy

At Precioussly, we take pride in delivering handcrafted resin jewellery. If you receive a damaged or wrong product, we offer a refund or replacement.

Eligible for Refund

  • Product is physically damaged during delivery.
  • Product received is different from the one ordered.

Not Eligible

  • Change of mind after receiving the product.
  • Damage after delivery due to misuse.
  • Request made after 48 hours of delivery.

Refund Process

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Send clear photo/video of the issue.
  • Team response within 24–48 hours.
  • Refund processed in 5–7 business days after approval.

Return Pickup

If eligible, we will arrange a reverse pickup. Product must be in original condition with packaging.
